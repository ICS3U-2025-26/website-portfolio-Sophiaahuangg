Arcade Corner: Cake Smashing Game

How to Play

Welcome to my custom p5.js arcade project! This game runs on a state engine that handles transitions from the main menu straight into custom difficulties.

  • Click the Start Game graphic button on the main title overlay screen.
  • Choose your speed setting: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Cakes will randomly pop up from the 6 ground holes across the canvas.
  • Left-click directly on the active cakes to smash them before they disappear!
  • Every cake that expires before you click it costs you one heart life (♥).
  • If you miss 3 cakes, the game over screen runs. Click "Play Again" to restart!

Pro-Tip: Hard mode speeds up the spawn clock and drops cake display life timers down to just 700 milliseconds!
