About Me

Hi! I’m Sophia, and I love spending time with my friends and family—they inspire me and make life fun every day. I’m passionate about rhythmic gymnastics, which challenges me to stay disciplined and creative, and I enjoy snowboarding as a way to explore the outdoors and stay adventurous. While I also like exploring coding and technology projects, my greatest joys come from the people I care about and the activities that make me feel energized and happy.
